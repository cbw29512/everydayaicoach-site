Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Plain English Summary

We built this app for older adults who deserve simple, honest answers. Here is exactly what we do and do not do with your information.

What We Collect

  • A random device identifier — this is a code that identifies your device so we can count your credits. It is not linked to your name or personal information.
  • Your credit balance — stored on your device and on our server to prevent fraud.
  • The text you type into buttons — sent to our AI service to generate your answer, then discarded. We do not store your questions.
  • Photos you take — sent to our AI service for analysis only, then immediately discarded. We never store your photos.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your location
  • Your contacts
  • Your browsing history
  • Any health information you type into the app

We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or third parties. Ever.

AI Service

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to generate responses. Your questions are sent to Anthropic's servers to generate answers. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing. We do not store your questions on our servers after the response is delivered.

In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed by Apple or Google. We do not see or store your payment information. Apple and Google's privacy policies apply to all purchases.

Medical Disclaimer

This app is for informational purposes only. It is not a medical service and does not replace professional medical advice. Always consult your doctor or pharmacist for health decisions.

Children

This app is designed for adults 50 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please email us at privacy@everydayaicoach.app
